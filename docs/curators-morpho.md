Merkl — the incentive layer used by Morpho curators — added MegaETH support in February 2026, a typical precursor to Morpho deployments. **Re7 Labs** is the most likely first curator mover when/if Morpho deploys on MegaETH, followed by Gauntlet.

## Part 2: French-Linked Curators

Morpho itself is a French project: the **Morpho Association** is registered under N°W751263773 at 24 rue de Clichy, 75009 Paris, the protocol's Terms of Use are governed by French law (Tribunal Judiciaire de Paris), and Morpho Labs has its main office in central Paris. Founders Paul Frambot, Mathis Gontier-Delaunay and others met at Télécom Paris / Polytechnique.

### French curator profiles

**Steakhouse Financial — French co-founder, Swiss legal entity.**
Co-founder Sébastien Derivaux is French and based in Strasbourg. The legal entity is incorporated in Zug, Switzerland. Steakhouse is the largest curator on Morpho with 48 vaults and ~$1.5B TVL. It curates Coinbase's USDC lending product, Apollo's institutional vault, and Fasanara's mF-ONE private credit vault. Sébastien Derivaux previously led MakerDAO's $2.2B allocation into US Treasuries. The Steakhouse Wrapped 2025 newsletter reports vaults grew +600% from January to $1.5 billion by December 2025.

**MEV Capital — French CEO, Lithuanian HQ.**
Co-founder and CEO Laurent Bourquin is French (ex-Société Générale CIB). Headquarters: Vilnius, Lithuania; second office in Downtown Dubai. MEV Capital was appointed by Société Générale-FORGE as on-chain asset manager for USDCV and EURCV stablecoins — making it the curator-of-record for the largest French TradFi bank's on-chain stablecoin program. Note: MEV Capital had $25.42 million in exposure in the November 2025 Stream Finance / xUSD collapse and is still rebuilding reputation.

**Stake DAO — French-founded.**
Founded by Julien Bouteloup, a French entrepreneur and Curve core-team member. Legal entity in Zug, Switzerland. Stake DAO formally entered curation via SDGP-58 ("Boost Stake DAO growth by launching a Curation Vertical") and curates Curve/Pendle LP-collateralized vaults on Morpho.

**Morpho Labs / Morpho Association (first-party).**
Headquartered in Paris (29 employees per PitchBook). Runs first-party curated vaults on newer chains where third-party curators have not yet onboarded (Base, Polygon, Unichain). Functionally a French in-house curator option on its own protocol.

### Non-French curators (for clarity)

- **Solana:** official risk contributor for Kamino Lend V2

**Gauntlet — institutional scale, aggressive chain expansion.**
Gauntlet manages ~$1.29–1.41B on Morpho and has expanded to:

Gauntlet's quant-risk model is well suited to high-throughput, low-latency chains.

**Steakhouse Financial — selective but proven on Solana.**
Steakhouse launched on Kamino in May 2025 and now curates 4 vaults on Solana, with Solana TVL in the multiple nine figures. Forward intent stated explicitly: "More on Solana." The firm is more selective with new chains than Re7 or Gauntlet — it waits for proven liquidity — but once committed, brings institutional-grade infrastructure and the Coinbase brand relationship.

**MEV Capital — broadest declared chain count.**
Claims 60+ vaults deployed across 10+ blockchains. Also appears in the RedStone December 2025 Solana lending curator roster alongside Steakhouse, Gauntlet, Re7, and Sentora.

**Sentora — quietly multi-chain.**

## Part 4: MegaETH — Current State and Curator Proximity

### What is MegaETH?

MegaETH is an Ethereum Layer 2 designed for real-time performance (sub-millisecond block times, 100,000+ TPS). It launched mainnet on January 22, 2026 and is positioned as high-frequency DeFi infrastructure — the chain Hyperliquid competes with on latency.

### DeFi protocols already live on MegaETH

- **Aave V3** — the dominant lending protocol on MegaETH; deposits surpassed **$575 million by May 1, 2026**
- **GMX** — perps (BTC/USD, ETH/USD, SOL/USD with up to 100x leverage, backed by USDm stablecoin liquidity)
- **World Markets** — unified spot + perps + undercollateralized lending platform (launched February 18, 2026)
- **USDM** — native stablecoin; circulating supply grew from ~$63M at TGE to $480M by May 7, 2026

### Morpho on MegaETH: not yet deployed

As of May 2026, **Morpho Blue has not deployed on MegaETH**. The current lending infrastructure is Aave V3. There is no public announcement on forum.morpho.org or the Morpho blog of a MegaETH deployment.

### The key leading indicator: Merkl is already there

**Merkl** — the incentive distribution layer used by Morpho in every multi-chain deployment and by its curators to run reward campaigns — added MegaETH to its list of supported chains in **February 2026**. This is the infrastructure prerequisite that typically precedes Morpho curator campaigns by 1–3 months on other chains.

### Which curators are closest to MegaETH?

No Morpho curator has announced a MegaETH vault. The ranking below is based on track record and chain-selection behavior:

**1. Re7 Labs — highest probability first-mover.**
Re7 has been the anchor curator on every major non-EVM or new-chain deployment in the Morpho ecosystem: first on Berachain (BEND), active on Solana (Kamino), and first to Starknet. They operate across 14 chains and have the research infrastructure to evaluate a new chain quickly. MegaETH's high-throughput architecture aligns with Re7's beta-heavy strategy profile (Gauntlet/Re7 keep 55–80% of capital in volatile assets).

**2. Gauntlet — strong second.**
Gauntlet was among the first to Kamino, runs vaults on Drift (Solana perps chain), and has explicit institutional mandate to expand. MegaETH's speed makes it attractive for quant-risk strategies. Gauntlet also already curates within the Aave ecosystem — and Aave is MegaETH's dominant lending protocol today — giving Gauntlet a natural on-ramp if/when Morpho deploys there.

**3. Steakhouse Financial — likely third, conditional on Coinbase.**
Steakhouse tends to follow institutional distribution rather than pioneer chains. The trigger for Steakhouse on MegaETH would be a Coinbase or Apollo integration, both of which are active Steakhouse partners. Coinbase has not announced MegaETH support as of this writing.

**4. MEV Capital — possible, given multi-chain mandate.**
MEV Capital's stated 10+ chain deployment count and Société Générale-FORGE mandate for stablecoin DeFi rails could extend to MegaETH, especially if SG-FORGE targets high-speed EVM environments. No public signal yet.

Once Morpho Blue ships on MegaETH, Re7 and Gauntlet will be the fastest to stand up a vault based on their track record on Berachain/Starknet/Kamino.

## Caveats

- **TVL figures are point-in-time snapshots.** Steakhouse, Sentora and Gauntlet rotate position-by-week. IOSG Ventures / Lagoon / DefiLlama figures cited above were taken in February 2026.
- **Curator list is not exhaustive.** Vault creation on Morpho is permissionless.
- **The November 2025 Stream Finance / xUSD event** caused significant losses across the curator industry. Stream disclosed a loss of approximately $93 million. Telos Consilium (TelosC) had the single largest curator exposure at $123.64 million; MEV Capital had $25.42 million; Re7 Labs and others were also affected. Steakhouse responded by seeding a $2.5M skin-in-the-game first-loss position. Curator-risk is real and recent.
- **Curator nationality is fuzzy.** "French" can mean French legal entity, French founder/CEO, or French operating team. Only the Morpho Association itself is unambiguously French by legal-entity definition; Steakhouse (Swiss entity), Stake DAO (Swiss entity), and MEV Capital (Lithuanian entity) all have non-French legal homes.
- **Multi-chain "expansion" data conflates "already live" with "openly interested."** Most public evidence is of curators that have already deployed on Kamino/Berachain/Starknet, not of curators with stated forward intent on other non-EVMs. Treat Solana/Berachain/Starknet findings as strong; absence of evidence on Sui/Aptos/Near/TON is inconclusive.
- **MegaETH proximity is forward-looking inference**, not confirmed plans. No curator has announced MegaETH vault deployment as of May 2026.
- **The Big Whale's Feb 2026 reporting** on Belem Capital potentially taking over parts of MEV Capital's business is a single-source secondary claim and should be re-verified before being acted on.
- **Armitage/Wintermute** was announced 2 days before this report's research date; details on collateral types, vault APY targets, and initial AUM had not been disclosed.
